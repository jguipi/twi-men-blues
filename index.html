Two men Blues

Our Story

Gilles & Jonathan are playing music together since 1998. Making them one of the longest lasting Blues duo in Canada. They are Two Men Blues.They had the privilege to play with some of the most prominent Canadian Blues musicians like Jimmy James, Stephen Barry, Guy Bélanger to name just a few. In the early years, an opportunity came up to meet with BB King, Muddy Waters and James Cotton, that motivated the duo to carry their music from Canada to the U.S.A and all around the Caribbean. From Mississippi to Chicago Blues, you can also enjoy classic hits from The Beatles, Jethro Tull, Bob Marley, Pink Floyd, Frank Sinatra, Johnny Cash, James Taylor, Neil Young, CCR, Eric Clapton and many more. All depending on your event.

Two Men Blues the perfect duo for your festival, restaurant, resort, bar, terrace, lounge event, wedding, private event, etc.
